## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Visit the Homepage**: Go to the CA Marketplace website
2. **Click "Register"**: Choose your account type:
   - **Client**: If you need accounting services
   - **Chartered Accountant**: If you're a CA offering services
3. **Fill in Your Details**:
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Name
   - Phone number
4. **Verify Your Email**: Check your inbox for a verification link
5. **Complete Your Profile**: Add additional details to help others find you

### Logging In

1. Click "Login" on the homepage
2. Enter your registered email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

## For Clients

### Finding the Right CA

#### Browse CA Directory

1. **Navigate to "Find CAs"** from the main menu
2. **Use Search & Filters**:
   - Search by name
   - Filter by specialization (GST, Income Tax, Audit, etc.)
   - Filter by experience level
   - Sort by hourly rate or rating
3. **View CA Profiles**:
   - Check their license number and verification status
   - Read their specializations and experience
   - View ratings and reviews from other clients
   - See their hourly rate and availability

#### Compare CAs

- Select multiple CAs to compare their:
  - Experience and qualifications
  - Hourly rates
  - Specializations
  - Client reviews and ratings

### Creating a Service Request

1. **From Your Dashboard**, click "Create New Request"
2. **Select Service Type**:
   - GST Filing
   - Income Tax Return
   - Audit
   - Accounting
   - Tax Planning
   - Financial Consulting
   - Company Registration
   - Other
3. **Fill in Details**:
   - Description of your requirement
   - Expected deadline (optional)
   - Estimated hours needed
4. **Select a CA** or let the system auto-assign
5. **Upload Documents** (if any)
6. **Submit Request**

### Managing Your Requests

#### View Request Status

Your requests can have these statuses:
- **PENDING**: Waiting for CA to accept
- **ACCEPTED**: CA has accepted your request
- **IN_PROGRESS**: Work has started
- **COMPLETED**: Service delivered
- **CANCELLED**: Request was cancelled

#### Track Progress

1. Go to "My Requests" in your dashboard
2. Click on any request to:
   - View details and timeline
   - See assigned CA information
   - Chat with your CA
   - Upload additional documents
   - Update requirements

### Communicating with Your CA

1. **Open Your Request**: Click on any active request
2. **Use the Chat Feature**:
   - Send messages in real-time
   - Share files and documents
   - Ask questions and get updates
3. **Get Notifications**: Receive alerts when your CA responds

### Making Payments

1. **When Service is Complete**: You'll receive a payment request
2. **Review the Amount**:
   - Service cost
   - Platform fee (10%)
   - Total amount
3. **Choose Payment Method**:
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - Net Banking
   - UPI
   - Wallet
4. **Complete Payment**: Follow the secure payment gateway steps
5. **Payment is Held in Escrow**: Released to CA after admin approval

### Leaving Reviews

After service completion:
1. **Rate Your Experience**: Give 1-5 stars
2. **Write a Review**: Share your feedback
3. **Submit**: Your review helps other clients make informed decisions

### Client Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:
- **Quick Stats**: Total requests, pending, in-progress, completed
- **Recent Requests**: Latest 5 service requests with status
- **Payment History**: Recent transactions
- **Notifications**: Important updates and messages

## For Chartered Accountants (CAs)

### Setting Up Your Profile

1. **Complete Registration** with:
   - CA License Number (unique)
   - Specializations (select all that apply)
   - Years of experience
   - Qualifications
   - Languages you speak
   - Your hourly rate
2. **Add a Professional Bio**: Describe your expertise
3. **Upload Documents**: License certificate, qualifications
4. **Submit for Verification**: Admin will verify your credentials

### Managing Service Requests

#### Viewing New Requests

1. Go to "My Requests" in your dashboard
2. See all requests assigned to you
3. Filter by status: Pending, Accepted, In Progress, Completed

#### Accepting Requests

1. **Review Request Details**:
   - Service type and description
   - Client requirements
   - Estimated hours and deadline
2. **Check Your Availability**
3. **Click "Accept"** to start working
4. **Or "Reject"** if you can't take it on

#### Working on Requests

1. **Update Status** as you progress:
   - Move to "In Progress" when you start
   - Mark "Completed" when done
2. **Communicate with Client**:
   - Use the chat feature for questions
   - Request additional documents if needed
   - Provide updates on progress
3. **Upload Deliverables**: Share completed work

### Managing Your Availability

1. Go to "Availability" in your profile
2. Set your available time slots:
   - Select dates
   - Choose time ranges (start and end time)
3. System auto-updates when you accept requests
4. Block dates when you're unavailable

### Earnings & Payments

#### Understanding Your Earnings

- **Total Earnings**: Lifetime earnings from all services
- **Pending Payments**: Payments awaiting admin release
- **Available Balance**: Amount in your wallet ready to withdraw

#### Payment Flow

1. **Client Pays**: After service completion
2. **Platform Deducts Fee**: 10% platform commission
3. **Your Amount**: 90% credited to your wallet
4. **Admin Releases**: After verification
5. **You Withdraw**: Request payout to your bank account

#### Withdrawing Funds

1. Go to "Wallet" or "Earnings"
2. Click "Request Payout"
3. Enter amount to withdraw
4. Select payout method:
   - Bank Transfer
   - UPI
   - RTGS/NEFT/IMPS
5. Enter bank details
6. Submit request
7. Wait for admin approval
8. Funds transferred within 2-3 business days

### CA Verification

**Why Verification Matters**:
- Builds client trust
- Get more visibility in search results
- Access premium features

**Verification Process**:
1. Submit complete profile with documents
2. Admin reviews your credentials
3. Verification status: Pending ‚Üí Verified/Rejected
4. Get verified badge on your profile

### CA Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:
- **Profile Completion**: Progress percentage
- **Active Requests**: Current work
- **Earnings Summary**: Total, pending, available
- **Recent Payments**: Latest transactions
- **Firm Management**: Register or view your firm (if applicable)

## For CA Firms

### Registering Your Firm

#### Step 1: Basic Information

1. Navigate to "Register Firm" in your CA dashboard
2. Fill in firm details:
   - **Firm Name** (unique)
   - **Firm Type**: Sole Proprietorship, Partnership, LLP, Private Limited
   - **Registration Number** (unique)
   - **GSTIN** (optional)
   - **PAN Number** (required)
   - **Contact Details**: Email, phone
   - **Address**: Full address with city, state, pincode
   - **Established Year**
   - **Website** (optional)
   - **Description**: About your firm
3. Click "Next"

#### Step 2: Invite Team Members

1. **Add CAs to Your Team**:
   - Enter CA email addresses
   - Assign roles for each:
     - **FIRM_ADMIN**: Full control over firm
     - **SENIOR_CA**: Senior chartered accountants
     - **JUNIOR_CA**: Junior chartered accountants
     - **SUPPORT_STAFF**: Administrative support
     - **CONSULTANT**: External consultants
   - Choose membership type:
     - Full-time
     - Part-time
     - Contractor
2. **Add Personal Message** (optional): Welcome note to team members
3. **Send Invitations**: CAs receive email invitations
4. Click "Next"

#### Step 3: Review & Submit

1. **Review All Details**: Check firm info and team members
2. **Submit for Verification**: Admin will review your firm
3. **Track Status**:
   - DRAFT: Not yet submitted
   - PENDING_VERIFICATION: Under admin review
   - ACTIVE: Verified and operational
   - SUSPENDED: Temporarily suspended
   - DISSOLVED: Permanently closed

### Managing Your Firm

#### Firm Dashboard

Access "My Firm" to see:
- **Firm Details**: Name, registration, contact info
- **Verification Status**: Current status with badge
- **Team Members**: List of all members with roles
- **Statistics**: Total requests, revenue, team size
- **Actions**: Edit details, manage members, view analytics

#### Adding More Team Members

1. Go to "My Firm"
2. Click "Invite Member"
3. Enter email and assign role
4. Send invitation
5. Track invitation status in "Invitations" section

#### Managing Invitations (As a CA)

If you receive a firm invitation:

1. Go to "Invitations" in your CA dashboard
2. View **Pending Invitations**:
   - See firm details
   - Your proposed role
   - Personal message from admin
3. **Accept** or **Reject** the invitation
4. View past invitations in "History" tab

### Independent Work Management

As a firm member, you can:

#### Request Independent Work Permission

1. Go to "Independent Work" section
2. Submit request with:
   - Client details
   - Service type
   - Estimated duration
3. Firm admin reviews for:
   - Conflict of interest
   - Time availability
   - Compliance with firm policies

#### Firm Policies

Firms can set policies:
- **NO_INDEPENDENT_WORK**: Not allowed
- **LIMITED_WITH_APPROVAL**: Requires approval per request
- **FULL_INDEPENDENT_WORK**: Freely allowed
- **CLIENT_RESTRICTIONS**: Allowed except for specific clients

#### Commission Sharing

If approved:
- You earn from independent work
- Firm may take a commission percentage
- All tracked in your earnings

### Firm Analytics

Firm admins can view:
- **Team Performance**: Individual member metrics
- **Revenue Breakdown**: By member, service type, month
- **Client Distribution**: Active clients per member
- **Utilization Rates**: Team workload and capacity
- **Growth Trends**: Month-over-month growth

## Payment & Billing

### How Payments Work

1. **Service Request Created**: Client and CA agree on scope
2. **Service Delivered**: CA completes work
3. **Client Pays**: Via secure payment gateway
4. **Escrow System**: Payment held by platform
5. **Admin Approval**: Verifies service completion
6. **Payment Release**: 90% to CA, 10% platform fee
7. **CA Wallet**: Funds available for withdrawal

### Payment Methods

**For Clients**:
- Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Debit Cards
- Net Banking
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
- Wallets (Paytm, Amazon Pay, etc.)

**For CA Payouts**:
- Bank Transfer (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS)
- UPI

### Payment Security

- **SSL Encrypted**: All transactions secure
- **Razorpay Integration**: PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway
- **Escrow Protection**: Funds held until service verified
- **Refund Policy**: Available for disputes

### Platform Fees

- **Client**: Pay full service amount
- **Platform Fee**: 10% of service cost
- **CA Receives**: 90% of service cost
- **No Hidden Charges**: Transparent pricing

### Tax Handling

- **TDS**: Deducted as per Income Tax Act
- **GST**: Applied on platform fee
- **Tax Certificates**: Available for download
- **Records**: Maintained for compliance

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is registration free?**
A: Yes, creating an account is completely free for both clients and CAs.

**Q: How long does verification take?**
A: CA verification typically takes 24-48 hours after document submission.

**Q: Can I change my profile information?**
A: Yes, you can edit your profile anytime from your dashboard.

### For Clients

**Q: How do I choose the right CA?**
A: Consider their specializations, experience, ratings, and reviews. Use our comparison tool to compare multiple CAs.

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the service?**
A: Contact support within 7 days of service completion. We'll review and may issue a refund.

**Q: Can I cancel a service request?**
A: Yes, you can cancel before the CA accepts it. After acceptance, contact the CA or support.

**Q: Is my payment secure?**
A: Yes, we use Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. Payments are held in escrow until service completion.

### For CAs

**Q: How do I get more clients?**
A: Complete your profile, get verified, maintain high ratings, and respond quickly to requests.

**Q: When will I receive payment?**
A: After service completion and admin approval, funds are credited to your wallet. You can withdraw anytime.

**Q: Can I work independently if I'm in a firm?**
A: Depends on your firm's policy. Request approval from your firm admin for independent work.

**Q: What if a client disputes my service?**
A: Contact support immediately. We'll mediate and review the case objectively.

### For Firms

**Q: How many members can I have?**
A: No limit! Invite as many CAs as your firm has.

**Q: Can members leave the firm?**
A: Yes, members can resign anytime. Pending work must be completed or reassigned.

**Q: What happens to ongoing requests if a member leaves?**
A: Requests can be reassigned to another team member or completed by the leaving member.
